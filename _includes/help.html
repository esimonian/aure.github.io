What You Need to View Files On This Website:

  • Portable Document Format (.pdf) files require Adobe Acrobat Reader
  • Finale files (.mus) require Finale or Finale Demo
  • Sibelius files (.sib) require Sibelius or Sibelius Demo
  • Scorch files require Sibelius Scorch, a free browser plug-in from Sibelius
  • Encore files (.enc) require G-VOX Encore
  • Cakewalk files (.wrk) require Cakewalk or Sonar
  • MIDI (.mid) files will run on most computers, but some systems may require a sequencer program.

We do indeed recommend the above-mentioned "Scorch", a fun and free program that lets you play the songfiles online without putting out the big bucks for a full notation software program. Install the plug-in, and watch the song play!
